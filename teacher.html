Jose Rizal as a Teacher

Life as a Teacher

Jose Rizal's life as a teacher was marked by his dedication to education and reform. After returning from Europe in 1887, he established a school in Manila called the La Liga Filipina. Rizal believed in the power of education to uplift his fellow Filipinos and promote social change. His teaching emphasized critical thinking, patriotism, and love for the country's culture and history. Rizal's methods focused on inspiring his students to question the status quo and strive for a better future.
